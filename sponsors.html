#HACK4ODISHA

Hey Geeks!


Hailing from the land of Odisha our team decided to conduct a one of it’s kind hackathon where individuals participating will be working on problem statements based on the state’s difficulties and future.

About HACK4ODISHA

The Hack4Odisha is one of the magnificent tech events that will be organized on DAY 5(20th-21st November ), of the GS Regional Summit. Come join us in our magnificent and grand Tech Journey – “Hack4Odisha”, in the GS Regional Summit for one whole day,i.e, 24 hours.
Note that: projects should revolve around Odisha in some or other way and thereby , they will be judge on the basis of Theme Impact, Video & Presentation, Idea & Creativity, and make sure that project is not plaigarised.


"Where we give the stage and you are the one who shines"

Rules

  • Each team should comprise 2-4 members.
  • It’s compulsory to have one girl member in every team.
  • Duration: 24 hours
  • The problem statement should be based or be centric on Odisha.

Perks of Participation

All participants will be awarded a participation certificate


Every participant will receive a 15% discount on a ticket to the India summit.


Apart from the certificate, WINNERS will be rewarded with :


  • Cash prizes
  • Free GirlScript India summit tickets
  • Swags & goodies
  • Other exciting perks and gifts
